    Fabulous series, Tom. Thank you for the great work; you've answered a lot of nagging little questions along the way. Please continue! One thought on the urls.py file. Since regex is often a sticking point, I've found it useful to provide a small comment right above each url, like this: # blog/ url(r'^$', views.list_of_posts, name='list_of_posts'), # blog/name-of-slug/ url(r'^(?P<slug>[-\w]+)/$', views.post_detail, name='post_detail'), # blog/category/category-slug/ url(r'^category/(?P<category_slug>[-\w]+)/$', views.list_of_posts_by_category, name='list_of_posts_by_category'), Just a thought.﻿
